Material Difference Technologies is a leading supplier of Prime, Wide-spec, and Reprocessed resins. Our resin distribution business supplies customers from numerous sites throughout North America. We have resin formulating and blending facilities with on-site analytical laboratories in Houston and in North Carolina

MDT company was founded on the concept of providing both distribution and resin compounding services to help our customers achieve the best cost outcomes. We can usually tune a product to your fit programs’ needs. Our technical staff will support your resin formula development, launch and quality assurance needs.

Material Difference Technologies’ “Value Add’ is to put our customers’ overall material requirements into our volume purchasing model to achieve cost savings. MDTs’ diversified services offer one stop shopping for Prime, Wide-spec and Reprocessed Resins.

MDT strives to purchase the highest quality raw materials for our distribution, compounding and reprocessing divisions. Our formulation development and post-blending processes ensure consistent material performance foe each load delivered. We validate that all our materials meet customer criteria and supply a Certificate of Analysis (COA) with each delivery.

Our products include certifications as FDA food-grade compliant, SCS Global Services recycled content certified, and PCR certified grade. We save millions of pounds of plastic each year from landfill and recycle them back into useful industrial and consumer products. MDT helps OEMs, large and small, to meet their recycled content and corporate sustainability goals.

MDT has received PNC letters for food-grade application approvals for both of our plastic reprocessing facilities. We have been certified by AM Testing, an ISO/IEC 17025:2017 Laboratory, as supplying post-consumer recycled (PCR) plastic materials. SCS Global Services has certified our Replas ReNew resin line as certified recycled plastic content. MDT is now reprocessing scrap HDPE and scrap TPO into recycled resin pellets for reuse in consumer and industrial applications. New Polypropylene formulated grades are being developed each month to meet the needs of customer projects.

Custom Compounding

Off Grade / Wide-spec Resin:
  • Our MDT Replas facility is a production and distribution facility for virgin and recycled resins.
  • OG/Wide-spec resins are non-prime resins, outside of the specific Prime spec ranges.
  • MDT can produce a wide variety of custom compounded and blended resins within consistent specifications to meet our processors’ requirements.
  • Prices offered will generally provide an advantage over prime resin usage.
  • MDT Replas has rail access, large silo capacity, and its own bulk truck fleet to serve customers in our region.
  • The laboratory and quality team at MDT Replas will perform ASTM testing services for your initial qualification process and ongoing delivery certification requirements.
Reprocessed Resin:
  • Both our MDT recycled resin facilities offer compounding and distribution of custom and standard MDT Repro grades.
  • These facilities offer full-service recycling of both post-consumer and post-industrial scrap.
  • Material conversion processes available are compounding, blending, shredding/grinding, and densifying of recycled scrap.
  • Standard grades offer high volume cost advantages while special formulations and toll processing fit your unique program requirements.
  • Customers experience cost savings through the use of post-industrial and post-consumer recycled scrap turned into recycled resins available at a discount to the cost of prime resins.
  • MDT Bromley provides rail, bulk truck, and boxed load products.
  • Full-scale ASTM laboratory testing and analysis is provided by our quality technicians for your initial qualification and ongoing delivery certification requirements.

Material Conversion / Toll Processing

MDT resin recycling facilities offer complete material conversion services to the recycled resin industry.

We offer scrap conversion of Polypropylene, HDPE and TPO materials into useful customer end-products.

Our conversion processes include:
  • Scrap material shredding
  • Grinding, as primary or secondary particle size reduction
  • Material densification
  • Scrap washing & separation
  • Extrusion (pelletizing)
MDT will deliver partial material conversion product or fully pelletized product to meet your needs.

If the scrap that you purchase or generate is not in the form you operation needs, please contact MDT to receive the necessary material conversion. ×
Post Blending

Post blending is a process where both compounded OG/Wide-spec or Pelletized recycled resin lots are uniformly mixed so that the spec-range of the delivered product is consistent throughout the entire load.

MDT’s large OG/Wide-spec silos can blend railcar-sized loads at one time.

Recycled resin lots are post-blended prior to final bagging or box-out. ×

Laboratory Analysis and Material R&D

MDT’s ASTM laboratories in both Houston, TX and Fletcher, NC provide full inspection reports for all incoming and outgoing loads.

FDA Approved Resins

Both our MDT Replas and MDT Bromley facilities offer FDA food-grade approved resin materials. MDT has received verified PNC letters from the FDA’s Center for Food Safety and Applied Nutrition.

MDT PureOlene FDA Grade Certified PIR-PP resins are available as natural, white, and black pellets.

Use of MDT FDA grades are approved for food contact articles, up to 100% recycled content. The FDA has approved these food grade resins for all Conditions of Use, A through J. Approved additives are allowed in our FDA grade resin materials.

These resins also carry certification by SCS Global Services as certified recycled content material. ×
